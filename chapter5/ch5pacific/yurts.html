The yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts finished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quiilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experince includes electiciry and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower abnd restroom facilities are located on the lodge.
What should I bring?
Most guests pack confortable walking shoes abd plan to dress dor changing weather with light layers of cloting. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
